# Executive Summary - Amman Digital Market Analytics

## Overview
This analysis reviews the performance of Amman Digital Market using active orders after removing cancelled orders and suspicious quantities. Five KPIs were calculated along with statistical tests and visualizations.

## Top Findings

1. Revenue grew very strongly by 151.7% in the most recent month compared to the previous month.
2. Books and Electronics are the top performing categories and together drive the largest portion of total revenue.
3. Amman accounts for 38.2% of total revenue, but customers in other cities have very similar average order values.

## Supporting Data

Finding 1 is supported by the Month-over-Month Revenue Growth KPI which reached +151.7%. See chart: 01_monthly_revenue_trend.png

Finding 2 is supported by the Revenue by Product Category KPI and the ANOVA statistical test (p-value = 0.0) which confirmed significant differences between categories. See charts: 02_revenue_by_category.png and 04_order_value_boxplot.png

Finding 3 is supported by the Average Order Value by City KPI and the Revenue Share by City chart. The t-test showed no significant difference (p-value = 0.41). See charts: 03_aov_by_city.png and 05_revenue_share_by_city.png

## Recommendations

1. Investigate what caused the 151.7% revenue growth in the latest month and apply the same strategies again to maintain the strong momentum.

2. Focus more inventory, promotions, and marketing budget on Books and Electronics since they are the strongest categories and show statistically significant differences in performance.

3. Expand marketing efforts to cities like Irbid, Zarqa, and Madaba because customer spending power is similar across all cities. Increasing order volume in these areas can grow total revenue without changing pricing strategy.
