# Executive Summary - Amman Digital Market Analytics

## Overview
This analysis evaluates the performance of the Amman Digital Market using transactional data from active orders after excluding cancelled transactions and invalid quantities. Five key performance indicators (KPIs) were developed, supported by statistical tests and visualizations to provide meaningful insights for decision-making.

## Top Findings

1. Revenue increased significantly by 151.7% in the most recent month, indicating strong growth momentum and a possible impact from seasonal demand or successful business strategies.

2. Books and Electronics are the top-performing categories, contributing the largest share of total revenue. Statistical testing (ANOVA, p-value ≈ 0.0) confirms that the differences between categories are statistically significant
3. Amman contributes the highest share of total revenue (38.2%), confirming its position as the main market hub. However, customers in other cities show similar spending behavior, as the t-test results (p-value = 0.19) indicate no statistically significant difference in average order value across cities

## Recommendations

1. Investigate the drivers behind the 151.7% revenue growth in the most recent month (e.g., promotions, campaigns, or seasonal demand) and replicate these strategies to sustain growth.

2. Focus marketing efforts, inventory planning, and promotional activities on Books and Electronics, as they are the strongest performing categories with statistically significant impact.

3. Expand marketing efforts to cities such as Irbid, Zarqa, and Madaba, as customer spending behavior is consistent across regions. Increasing order volume in these areas can significantly boost total revenue without changing pricing strategies
