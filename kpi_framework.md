# KPI Framework - Amman Digital Market

Define 5 KPIs for the Amman Digital Market. At least 2 must be time-based and 1 must be cohort-based.

---

## KPI 1

- **Name:** Total Revenue
- **Definition:** Total revenue generated from all completed orders after excluding cancelled orders and suspicious quantities greater than 100.
- **Formula:** SUM(quantity * unit_price)
- **Data Source (tables/columns):** order_items (quantity, product_id), orders (order_id, status), products (unit_price)
- **Baseline Value:** 48,701.50 JOD
- **Interpretation:** This KPI represents the overall business performance and total income generated from the platform.

---

## KPI 2

- **Name:** Average Order Value by City
- **Definition:** Average revenue per order segmented by customer city (cohort-based KPI).
- **Formula:** AVG(quantity * unit_price) GROUP BY city
- **Data Source (tables/columns):** order_items, orders, products, customers (city)
- **Baseline Value:** Order values are relatively similar across all cities with slight variation.
- **Interpretation:** Customers across different cities exhibit similar spending behavior, meaning growth should focus on increasing volume rather than pricing differences.

---

## KPI 3

- **Name:** Monthly Revenue Trend
- **Definition:** Total revenue aggregated by month to track performance over time (time-based KPI).
- **Formula:** SUM(quantity * unit_price) GROUP BY order_month
- **Data Source (tables/columns):** order_items, orders (order_date), products
- **Baseline Value:** Revenue shows a stable pattern with a sharp increase in the most recent month.
- **Interpretation:** The business experienced a strong spike in revenue recently, indicating possible successful campaigns or seasonal effects.

---

## KPI 4

- **Name:** Month-over-Month Revenue Growth Rate
- **Definition:** Percentage change in revenue from one month to the next (time-based KPI).
- **Formula:** ((current month revenue - previous month revenue) / previous month revenue) * 100
- **Data Source (tables/columns):** Derived from Monthly Revenue Trend
- **Baseline Value:** +151.68% in the latest month
- **Interpretation:** This indicates a significant surge in business performance and rapid short-term growth.

---

## KPI 5

- **Name:** Revenue by Product Category
- **Definition:** Total revenue generated by each product category (cohort-based KPI).
- **Formula:** SUM(quantity * unit_price) GROUP BY category
- **Data Source (tables/columns):** order_items, orders, products (category)
- **Baseline Value:** Books (~11,274 JOD) and Electronics (~11,005 JOD) generate the highest revenue.
- **Interpretation:** These categories are the main revenue drivers and should be prioritized in business strategy.

---

## Statistical Validation

**ANOVA Test - Order Value by Product Category**  
H0: Mean order value is the same across all categories  
H1: At least one category has a different mean  
Result: p-value = 2.11e-52 → Significant differences exist between categories.  
Interpretation: Product category has a strong impact on revenue, and differences between categories are statistically meaningful.

---

**T-Test - Revenue in Amman vs Other Cities**  
H0: Mean revenue is the same in Amman and other cities  
H1: Mean revenue is different  
Result: p-value = 0.195 → No significant difference.  
Interpretation: Although Amman generates higher total revenue, the average spending behavior is not significantly different from other cities.
